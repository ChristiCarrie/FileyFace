Turn Chaos Into Clarity

FileyFace saves people from having a million miscellaneous files in their downloads folder, by using Artificial Intelligence to sort and move downloaded files into folders they belong in. With just one click, you can save yourself time and hassle by letting us take care of organising your files.

Check us out on GitHub:

https://github.com/jaisenGT/FileyFace.git

FileyFace was conceived at a late-night dinner/brainstorming session between the team. We were going through our computers and saw one of our teammate's downloads folder. It was crazy, absolutely everything was in that folder; homework, personal projects, video downloads, random pictures, VS Code, and more. We were laughing about it, and the idea suddenly came to us! An application that could prevent the clutter by addressing the root cause: the initial download.

Our application focuses on leveraging OpenAI and TextRazor APIs to immediately place a newly downloaded file into its best-suited folder. We use OpenAI to generate the final prediction score for each folder or if any should be created and TextRazor to parse keywords from each document. It engages with the Operating System in order to move, create, and rename files, and has been heavily tested with the Windows Operating System. FileyFace supports multiple file formats, including .pdf, .docx, .pptx, .txt, and more! We also use multiple of Python's libraries, including but not limited to: os, shutil, sqlite3, tkinter, pathlib, and watchdog. We are combining AI, OS, and Tech Stack to determine the intended folder location of a downloaded file, right when it's first downloaded.
