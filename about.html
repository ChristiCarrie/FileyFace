About Us

We are Aadit Bansal, Oliver Lee, Jaisen Soundar, and Christina Wu, a team of four friends who are undergraduates at the Georgia Institute of Technology. We brainstormed FileyFace and implemented it during the 2024 HackGT hackathon.

Aadit is a Computer Science major with threads in Devices/Intel, and a Robotics minor! Oliver is also a Computer Science major with threads in Devices/Intel, and a BME minor! Jaisen is a Computer Science major with threads in Theory/Intel, and a minor in Biology! Christina is a Computer Science major with threads in People/Intel, and a minor in HMED!
